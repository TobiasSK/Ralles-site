MOONCAR

Short film 10 Minutes

Producer, Scriptwriter, Editor

Mooncar is a story about dreams. The two brothers Mikael and Kim live their lives uninterrupted in a little province in Denmark. Kim has no need to leave his hometown, however something is different for the younger brother Mikael. His dream project is to fly away and out into the sky, however serious events disrupts his attempts and he becomes physical locked in the little town. Together they tell the story of brotherhood and the hunt for what seems impossible.

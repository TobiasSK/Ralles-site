Rasmus Skjødt is a filmmaker based in Aarhus Denmark. He is specialized in storytelling across platforms from Multiplatform Storytelling & Production. It doesn’t matter which format or platform he must work with, he believes that there always is a good story to tell and he is eager to find it. Rasmus possesses the knowledge of most roles in a production team which makes it easier for him and others to speak together and cooperate to create the best work and to always try and raise the bar.

SMILE

Music Video 4 Minutes

Director, Editor

Smile is a film made for the band Wizard of Love. From the idea of a 70’s California vibe we wanted to create a trippy music video that expressed a felling and vibe through blended images and the old-school camera feel. It was all shot on old Canon tape recorders which led to a very different and challenging workflow.

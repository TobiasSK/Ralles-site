NORDIC NOIR

Moodfilm 3 Minutes

Producer, Cinematographer, Scriptwriter, Editor

Nordic Noir is a project made in collaboration with Filmby Aarhus. We were given the task to go to the Danish west coast and from our own perspective and ideas of the place, tell multiple stories that would show different themes and moods from the west coast. The final project turned out like this and was used in presentations to different productions companies in Denmark and Germany to show them the Danish west coast.
